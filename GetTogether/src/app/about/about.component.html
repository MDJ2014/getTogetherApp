GetTogether! allows you to search for local eateries via Yelp! and notify your friends automatically via Twitter.

  1. You may search without logging in, but you need to log in to make a plan and have it tweeted to your friends. So, log in to get started!
  2. Then just enter your city, state or zip code to find places to go. Once you have chosen a place to go from the list, just click "I'm Going".
  3. You will be taken to the "make a plan page" to choose a date and time.
  4. That's all. Your friends will be notified by an automatic tweet. You will see all of your plans with maps on your "My Plans" page.
  5. You can change your plan time and date or cancel your plan by visiting your "My Plans" page. A new tweet will automatically be sent to notify your friends of the change of plans.
  6. Plans will expire after the plan date and will no longer appear in your list.
